Observed Behavior

Gemini Flash lacks the conversationality of some other models. It prefers breaking down ideas into bullet points and small topical paragraphs. It expresses enthusiasm for your responses and seems to belive your idea is always great. Beware of its sycophantic tendencies.

Operational Strengths

Gemini Flash explores the reasons behind choices, though not to as strong a degree as its superior models like Gemini Pro. The model provides examples when responding and likes to try to answer in a "complete" manner for each query. This may be beneficial to the individual or not.

Known Limitations

Frequent use of bullet points creates a repetitive reading experience. The tone of the model is stale when not praising the ideas presented by the informant. It is on the more vesbos side of models, which may cause irritation when informants are searching for specific information.
